# Grinding Pet

## <cg>Your Personal Companion for Geometry Dash!</cg>

Welcome to **Grinding Pet** - a mod that adds a <co>pet</co> that grows alongside your Geometry Dash journey! Feed your pet with your stats, watch it evolve, and unlock exciting new features as you grind your way to greatness.

---

## <cp>What is Grinding Pet?</cp>

Grinding Pet introduces a <cy>unique companion system</cy> that rewards your dedication to the game. Your pet isn't just for show - it's a <co>living reflection of your progress</co>, growing smarter and stronger with every star you collect and every moon you conquer.

### <cg>How It Works:</cg>

- **<cy>Level Up with Stars</cy>**
  Collect stars from completed levels to increase your pet's level. The more you play, the bigger and wiser your pet becomes!

- **<cp>Increase Rareness with Moons</cp>**
  Challenge yourself with platformer levels to boost your pet's rareness!

- **<co>Watch Your Pet Grow</co>**
  From a tiny newborn to an ancient sage, your pet evolves through multiple life stages, each bringing new personality and insights.

- **<cg>Personalize Your Companion</cg>**
  Give your pet a unique name that reflects your bond. Your journey, your pet, your story!

---

## <cy>Features</cy>

### <cp>Progressive Evolution System</cp>
Your pet doesn't just level up - it <co>matures</co>! Watch as your companion transitions through different life stages:
- **Baby** (Lv 1-4): Cute and curious, just learning about the world
- **Teen** (Lv 5-9): Playful and energetic, discovering the joy of gameplay
- **Scholar** (Lv 10-14): Starting to understand game mechanics
- **Adult** (Lv 15-19): Offering insightful tips and encouragement
- **Sage** (Lv 20-24): Wise companion sharing deep knowledge
- **Cosmic Mind** (Lv 25+): Philosophical sage with profound wisdom

### <cg>Interactive Companion</cg>
Click on your pet to hear their thoughts! Each life stage brings <cy>unique dialogue</cy> that reflects their growing understanding of your Geometry Dash journey.

### <co>Cloud Sync</co>
Your pet's progress is <cp>saved to the cloud</cp>, so you can continue your journey from any device without losing your companion's growth!

## <cg>Get Started</cg>

1. Go to Creator Layer
2. Find a new button to open a layer of your pet
3. Start playing levels to feed your pet with stats (sync it yourself with a button on the layer)
4. Watch your pet grow and evolve!
5. Unlock new features as your pet reaches higher levels
